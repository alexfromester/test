A Third Way

We live in uncertain times. The UK Purchasing Managers index has fluctuated wildly in the last 18 months or so from 32 in April 2020 to 66 in May 2021. 50 is stable in the sense that there is neither under or over-confidence in managers making purchasing decisions. Who knows where it will be in the future? Next month? Next year? A careful director spends wisely and keeps something aside for a rainy day.

Don’t go just yet! I’m getting to the good stuff.

Why do I introduce myself with the dull old PMI figures? Because I am here to offer a solution: for your web design solutions (or put here whatever you want to clearly offer), you can go with a corporate or a boutique offer, but you know it is going to be pricy.

The usual alternative is hunting down a freelancer or group of freelancers. But will they do a good job? Can you trust them?

I’m here to offer a Third Way.

Outside my CEO persona, I’m a solution seeker.
I see the problem above so I see an opportunity for a Third Way in these uncertain times.

I’m here to help and connect UK-based companies with Estonian and Eastern-European professionals. From offshore to any on-side opportunities, I’m happy to discuss onboarding options for hiring our in-house professionals to help your business grow.

Is this the missing part of the puzzle you were looking for? I do hope if you are prospecting for professional teams or niche-oriented professionals without “The Big Contract” that I can help.
